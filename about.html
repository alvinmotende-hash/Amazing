About Us

The Motende family is a circle of love and resilience, each member adding their own unique strength to the whole. Irene Mokua, the mother, is the heart of the family — her care, wisdom, and guidance provide stability and warmth, ensuring traditions and values are passed down. Josphat Motende, the late father, is remembered with deep respect for the strength and protection he offered, and his legacy continues to inspire his children. Joyllete Nyarangi, the firstborn, carries the mantle of responsibility and leadership, often serving as a role model for her siblings. Martha Gesare, the second born, brings empathy and kindness, acting as a mediator and a source of compassion within the household. Alvin Motende, the youngest, adds creativity and energy, his innovative spirit and enthusiasm bringing joy and fresh ideas to the family. Together, they form a bond that honors the past, celebrates the present, and looks forward to the future with unity and hope.
