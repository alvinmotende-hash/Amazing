Welcome to our family website!

The Motende family is built on love, resilience, and shared memories. At its heart is Irene Mokua, the mother whose care and guidance keep the household strong, and the late Josphat Motende, remembered with deep respect for the wisdom and stability he provided. Their children — Joyllete Nyarangi, the firstborn who carries the mantle of responsibility, Martha Gesare, the second born with her own unique strengths, and Alvin Motende, the youngest whose energy brings joy — each contribute to the family’s story in their own way. Together, they form a bond that honors the past, celebrates the present, and looks forward to the future with unity and hope.

Joyllete Nyarangi Nyanduko

Joyllete Nyarangi, the firstborn in the family, carries a special role as the eldest child. Often seen as a guiding figure for her younger siblings, she represents responsibility and leadership within the household. Her position naturally places her at the forefront of family traditions and milestones, making her a source of pride and inspiration for both her parents and siblings.

Martha Gesare Nyarangi

Martha Gesare Nyarangi, the second child in the family, brings a unique perspective and energy to our household. Known for her kindness and empathy, she often serves as a mediator during family discussions and is deeply involved in community service activities. Her dedication to helping others makes her a beloved member of our family.

Alvin Nyarangi Motende

Alvin Nyarangi Motende, the third child in the family, is known for his creativity and innovative spirit. He often comes up with unique solutions to problems and is a natural leader among his peers. His enthusiasm and positive attitude contribute significantly to the family's dynamic and success.

Irene Mokua Nyakerario

Irene Mokua holds the heart of the family together, embodying care, resilience, and guidance. As the mother, she nurtures each child with love while balancing the responsibilities of family life. Her role extends beyond daily routines — she is the source of wisdom, comfort, and strength, ensuring that traditions and values are passed down. Irene’s presence provides stability and warmth, making her the cornerstone of the household.

Josphat Nyarangi Motende

Josphat Motende, the father of the family, is remembered with deep respect and love. As the guiding figure, he provided strength, wisdom, and stability, shaping the values that continue to hold the family together. His presence brought warmth and protection, and even in his absence, his legacy lives on through the lessons he instilled and the love he shared. He remains a cherished part of the family’s story, honored in memory and carried forward in the lives of his children.
